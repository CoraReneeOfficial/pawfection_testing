# Gemini AI Assistant Capabilities

The Gemini AI Assistant is equipped with a variety of tools to manage your pet grooming business efficiently. Below is a full list of her abilities:

## Client & Pet Directory
- **Get Owners:** Retrieve a list of owners and their details.
- **Add Owner:** Create a new owner profile.
- **Edit Owner:** Update an existing owner's information.
- **Delete Owner:** Remove an owner from the system.
- **Get Dogs:** Retrieve a list of dogs to find their details or IDs.
- **Add Dog:** Register a new dog to an owner.
- **Edit Dog:** Update an existing dog's profile (e.g., breed, weight, notes).
- **Delete Dog:** Remove a dog from the system.

## Schedule & Appointments
- **Sync Calendar:** Manually trigger a synchronization of appointments with the connected Google Calendar.
- **Get Appointments:** View scheduled appointments for a specific date or retrieve details of a specific appointment.
- **Get Daily Appointment Count:** Check the total number of appointments scheduled for a specific date.
- **Add Appointment:** Book a new appointment for a dog, selecting the date, time, groomer, and services. *(Automatically syncs with Google Calendar and sends email/text notifications to the customer).*
- **Edit Appointment:** Modify the details of an existing appointment. *(Automatically updates Google Calendar and sends an updated notification).*
- **Delete Appointment:** Cancel and remove an appointment. *(Automatically removes from Google Calendar and sends a cancellation notification).*
- **Get Current Time:** Fetch the current date and time in the store's local timezone.

## Business Management
- **Get Groomers:** Retrieve a list of available groomers/staff.
- **Get Services:** Retrieve a list of services offered and their details.
- **Add Service:** Create a new service offering (e.g., "Nail Trim").
- **Get Store Info:** View the business's configuration and details.
- **Update Store Info:** Modify the store's information (name, phone, address, timezone).
- **Get Revenue:** View the total revenue for a specific date (Admin/Superadmin only).

## Custom Communications
- **Send Custom Email:** Send a custom email message to a specific customer. Gemini will ask for your message, draft a professional version, and allow you to choose which one to send.
- **Send Custom Text:** Send a custom SMS text message to a specific customer. Gemini will ask for your message, draft a professional version, and allow you to choose which one to send.
